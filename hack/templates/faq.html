Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've got answers.

What is the purpose of this app?

to provide a seamless and convenient platform for reserving and accessing lockers to store your belongings securely.

How do I reserve a locker?

simply open the app, browse the available lockers, select the one you want, and confirm your reservation.

How can I access my reserved locker?

after reserving a locker, you will receive a unique access code or NFC token to open your locker using the app.

Can I change my locker reservation?

yes, you can modify your locker reservation details, such as the date and time, by accessing the app and making the necessary changes.

Is my personal information secure?

we prioritize the security and privacy of your personal information. our app follows strict security protocols and encryption standards to safeguard your data.
